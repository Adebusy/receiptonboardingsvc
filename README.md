**ReceiptMe — Product Overview**

**Overview******
ReceiptMe is an AI-powered, mobile-first application designed to help small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) generate professional receipts quickly and effortlessly.
The app simplifies the entire receipt creation process by allowing business owners to complete a one-time onboarding, after which receipts can be generated in seconds using natural language. ReceiptMe eliminates manual calculations, repetitive data entry, and unprofessional receipt formats commonly faced by SMEs.

Key Features & Workflow
1. Business Onboarding
During onboarding, business owners securely provide:
* Business and owner details
* Company name and address
* Company logo (optional upload or AI-assisted suggestion)
* Authorized signature (e.g., CEO or owner signature)
These details are stored once and reused automatically for all future receipts.

2. AI-Assisted Branding Setup
ReceiptMe uses AI (via an MCP-powered LLM service) to:
* Suggest company logo concepts based on the business name (optional)
* Recommend professional receipt templates suitable for the business type
Users retain full control by selecting their preferred logo and receipt template, which are then saved to their company profile.

3. Intelligent Receipt Generation
Once onboarding is complete, users can generate receipts by simply describing a transaction in plain language (text or voice), such as:
“Generate a receipt for John: 10 notebooks at £2 each and 3 pens at £1.50.”
The AI engine extracts structured receipt data (items, quantities, unit prices), while the backend system:
* Calculates totals accurately
* Applies currency and optional tax rules
* Populates the selected receipt template
* Attaches the company logo and authorized signature

4. Professional PDF Output
ReceiptMe automatically generates a clean, professional PDF receipt that includes:
* Business branding
* Itemized purchase details
* Accurate totals
* Date and receipt reference
* Authorized signature
The PDF format ensures receipts are compliant, shareable, and suitable for record-keeping.

5. WhatsApp-First Delivery
Receipts can be instantly:
* Downloaded as PDF
* Shared directly with customers via WhatsApp
This WhatsApp-first approach aligns with how most SMEs communicate with customers, reducing friction and speeding up transactions.

Problem Solved
ReceiptMe addresses common SME challenges:
* Manual and error-prone receipt writing
* Lack of professional branding
* Time wasted on repetitive calculations
* Difficulty sharing receipts digitally
* Complex accounting software unsuitable for small businesses
By combining AI assistance with a simple user experience, ReceiptMe enables SMEs to create professional receipts without accounting knowledge or technical expertise.

Value Proposition
ReceiptMe enables small businesses to move from sale to professional receipt in seconds — using simple language and WhatsApp delivery.

Target Users
* Sole traders and freelancers
* Small retail shops
* Informal and micro-businesses
* SMEs operating in WhatsApp-centric markets
